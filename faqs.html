Frequently Asked Questions

When Was I AM XIS Born?

I AM XIS was established by Adekoya Okikiola Christopher in May, 2020.

What Does I AM XIS Mean?

I AM XIS means I AM CHRIS.

What Is I AM XIS All About?

I AM XIS is a lifestyle platform. We promote living a fulfilled life through our curated shop filled with our most-loved items made by us.

What Is The I AM XIS SHOP?

I AM XIS SHOP was established in 2021. Our shop comprises hair and skin care essentials, beauty, and wardrobe must-haves.

What Is I AM XIS SHOP Integrated With?

Our shop is integrated with Paystack for easier accessibility and faster checkouts.

Do you offer product exchanges?

Generally, we don't offer exchanges. However, we may if you receive a damaged product.

Are Beauty Products In I AM XIS SHOP Safe To Use?

All beauty products are safely produced and free from parabens, mineral oil, sulfates, and silicones.

Which countries do you ship to?

We ship only to specified addresses in Nigeria. We hope to ship worldwide soon.

How Many Days Does Delivery Take?

Deliveries will take approximately 1-5 business days.

Why is my order taking so long?

Kindly bear with us. There might have been an issue or two with logistics.

I received a damaged product. What should i do?

We're so sorry this happened. Kindly send us a picture of the damaged product you received via email at care@theiamxis.com.ng or Whatsapp at 0708 005 4074, using your order number as the subject/title. Afterward, an exchange based on this condition will be facilitated for you.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept direct bank transfers and all major debit cards.

I emailed customer service but haven't gotten a reply. Why?

We might have missed your email due to unforeseen circumstances. Kindly hold on. We'll get back to you as soon as possible.

Do i need an account to buy from from I AM XIS?

You don't need an account to purchase your favorite items from us.

When will my account be billed?

Your account is billed after you place your order on our shop site.

I'm having issues making payments. What should i do?

Kindly contact us by email at care@theiamxis.com.ng or via call at 0708 005 4074.

Can i amend or cancel my order?

Unfortunately, amendments or cancellations of orders cannot be made after placing your order.
