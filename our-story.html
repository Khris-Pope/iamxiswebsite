OUR STORY

How We Started & Where We Are
Today

2020

I AM XIS by Adekoya Okikiola Christopher started during the Coronavirus Pandemic.

I AM XIS means I AM CHRIS. Chris's Dad inspired our name. He often used XIS instead of Chris in his text messages. So, to honor our founder's name and meaning, we chose XIS as our company name.

As bearers of Christ, we're committed to using our creativity and innovation to help people become their best selves. Our goal is to shine the light of Christ through our work and make a beneficial difference in the world.

In the heat of the pandemic, we released our debut article titled "6 Amazing Tips for Thicker and Longer Natural Hair". It featured one of Chris's favorite natural hair influencers, Evani Hawkins. Additionally, we produced further content covering haircare, skincare, and wellness.

2021

We were thrilled to launch our first online store, offering a carefully curated selection of beauty products including shampoos, conditioners, leave-ins, face masks, and more.

However, we, unfortunately, experienced a setback when we lost our databases. After taking some time to regroup, we returned in 2022 with even more fantastic items in our shop, as well as fresh and exciting new content for our customers to enjoy.

2022

Throughout our journey, we underwent a process of self-discovery. To enhance the customer experience and streamline checkout, we migrated our online store to our Paystack Storefront. The results have been phenomenal.

Our newest brand, I AM XIS LABEL (formerly I AM XIS Apparel), made a splash with our first offering, the NO. 18 Label T-Shirt, which sold out in no time, proving its undeniable popularity. In addition, our tote bag collections have been our top-selling products since their release.

2023

Our company has recently introduced four new brands: I AM XIS HAIR, SKIN, LABEL, and BEAUTY. These brands embody our core values, passions, and commitments.

We're currently working on some exciting new collections and still taking orders for haircare and skincare items. But our main priority is the development of the I AM XIS LABEL.
