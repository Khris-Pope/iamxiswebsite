OUR STORY

How We Started & Where We Are
Today

Elevating Everyday Essentials

At I AM XIS, we believe that everyday essentials should be anything but ordinary. We craft totes, mugs, and goods that blend functionality with a touch of elegance—made for those who value style in simplicity.

2020

I AM XIS was born from a passion for minimalism and timeless design during the Coronavirus Pandemic. Founded by Adekoya Okikiola Christopher, the brand’s name carries personal significance. "I AM XIS" means "I AM CHRIS," a tribute to Chris’s father, who often used “XIS” in his text messages instead of Chris. To honor this unique connection, we chose XIS as our company name.

At I AM XIS, our journey began with a simple idea: to offer high-quality, beautifully designed products that fit effortlessly into any lifestyle. From our first tote bag to our latest collection of mugs, we have stayed true to this vision of blending functionality with elegance, designed for those who value style in simplicity.

As bearers of Christ, we are driven by a commitment to use creativity and innovation to help people become their best selves. Our goal is to reflect the light of Christ through our work and make a positive difference in the world. This dedication began in the heat of the pandemic, with our debut article "6 Amazing Tips for Thicker and Longer Natural Hair," featuring Chris’s favorite natural hair influencer, Evani Hawkins. Since then, we’ve expanded our content to cover haircare, skincare, and wellness—all with the same thoughtful, intentional approach that defines our brand.

2021

We were thrilled to launch our first online store, offering a carefully curated selection of beauty products including shampoos, conditioners, leave-ins, face masks, and more.

However, we, unfortunately, experienced a setback when we lost our databases. After taking some time to regroup, we returned in 2022 with even more fantastic items in our shop, as well as fresh and exciting new content for our customers to enjoy.

2022

Throughout our journey, we’ve embraced self-discovery and continuous improvement. To enhance your shopping experience and offer a seamless checkout process, we’ve migrated our store to our Paystack Storefront for ease and convenience. The results have been phenomenal.

Our newest brand, I AM XIS LABEL (formerly I AM XIS Apparel), made a splash with our first offering, the NO. 18 Label T-Shirt, which sold out in no time, proving its undeniable popularity. In addition, our tote bag collections have been our top-selling products since their release.

2023

Our company has recently introduced four new brands: I AM XIS HAIR, SKIN, and LABEL. These brands embody our core values, passions, and commitments.

2024

We proudly launched our latest collection, I AM XIS GOODS, showcasing mesmerizing glossy mugs, stunning prints, and a custom-made variant. Mid-year, we unveiled our magic mugs—featuring glossy interiors, a sleek matte finish, enchanting designs, and a custom-made variant as well. Discover these captivating pieces and more, now available in our shop page.
